iPhoneStudio.app

After the launch of the 2020 iPhones, Apple introduced a tool on their website called MagSafe Studio to let consumers see what their new phones would look like with different cases and different MagSafe accessories. I found it extremely useful and fun, and was able to order my new phone accessories knowing it would look exactly how I liked it. Unfortunately Apple didn’t update the tool for the 2021 iPhones, and after searching the internet, couldn’t find a single tool like it. So I made one.

I started working on iPhoneStudio.app the day before preorders started, and had a rough working version ready for preorder day. I spent the following few days updating, tweaking, and perfecting the user interface and user experience, and then later in the week a reddit post featuring my iPhoneStudio.app went semi-viral, and a tech blog from the Czech Republic wrote an article featuring my site as well. Within two weeks the website had over 13,000 visits, and I’m really happy that I was able to help so many people pick out their new phone accessories.
